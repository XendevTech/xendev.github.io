How May We Help You?

Web Apps

Let Xendev streamline your online presence. We can build your business anything from simple static portfolios to large and dynamic E-Commerce platforms.

Mobile Apps

Need a mobile app for your business? Our engineers have you covered! Xendev can build mobile and tablet applications for your business solutions.

Cloud Computing

Let us migrate your online presence to Amazon AWS. We'll manage your data and secure your information, allowing you to offer more secure services to your customers.

Data Analytics

So you've got data? But what does it mean? Xendev can help you analyze your data so your business can streamline processes for maximum returns.

Database Management

Have a lot of data? Need to collect more? Or perhaps your business needs help determining how to store your data. Xendev can help handle that data.

IT Services

Do you need managed Information Technology solutions for your business? Let Xendev's IT technicians free your time to focus on your customers needs.

About

The story of Xendev

  • January 2011

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Xendev started as Cre8, a small web development and marketing studio in Springfield, MO.

  • July 2014

    The Big Move

    Cre8 moved to Florida and expanded services to include cloud computing and databasing solutions.

  • July 2016

    Name Change

    Cre8 becomes Xendev! Along with the new identity came new services: Managed IT solutions for small businesses.

  • January 2018

    More Services for You

    Xendev expanded its services to include Mobile App Development and Data Analytics consulting.

  • Be Part
    Of Our
    Story!
